Firebird Frankie Foundry was created in 2018 as a creative outlet for Sonja Hanekom. Since then she has moved her design focus more to the online gaming community and social media content creator community. Be it a simple Logo, a profesional Branding Package or a casual Twitch Emote, she enjoys the creation and collaboration on every project. She has also started creating hand drawn digital artworks for clients for their marketing needs or even just for them to enjoy as pieces of art.
